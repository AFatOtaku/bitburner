比特节点-4：奇点

源文件级别：0 / 3



难度：普通



奇点已经到来。人类已经消失了，取而代之的是机器多于人类的人工超级智能。

在这个BitNode中，进展明显困难。所有状态的经验获得率都降低了。大多数挣钱的方法现在都会明显减少。

在这个比特节点中，你将获得一套新的Netscript功能，称为奇点(Singularity)功能。这些功能允许你通过脚本来控制游戏的大部分方面，包括为派别/公司工作，购买/安装增强器，以及创建程序。

摧毁这个比特节点会给你源文件4，如果你已经有了这个源文件，它会将它的等级提升到最高3级。这个源文件可以让你访问和使用其他比特节点的奇点功能。这个源文件的每一级都会减少奇点功能的内存成本。


BitNode-4: The Singularity

Source-File Level: 0 / 3



Difficulty: normal



The Singularity has arrived. The human race is gone, replaced by artificially superintelligent beings that are more machine than man.

In this BitNode, progressing is significantly harder. Experience gain rates for all stats are reduced. Most methods of earning money will now give significantly less.

In this BitNode you will gain access to a new set of Netscript Functions known as Singularity Functions. These functions allow you to control most aspects of the game through scripts, including working for factions/companies, purchasing/installing Augmentations, and creating programs.

Destroying this BitNode will give you Source-File 4, or if you already have this Source-File it will upgrade its level up to a maximum of 3. This Source-File lets you access and use the Singularity Functions in other BitNodes. Each level of this Source-File reduces the RAM cost of singularity functions
